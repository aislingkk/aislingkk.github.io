research question

What is the stock market of Chinese Internet listed companies?

Description Protocol Data

Description

In the Visualization, the color of pink purple represented the China Stock Exchange Market (HKG, SHE, SHA), and the color of blue-green stated the U.S. Stock Exchange Market(NASDAQ, NYSE). The outer ring pie chart presented the percentage of each stock exchange. Besides, each line connected each company name with the stock exchange market in details, and the text could help to describe and supplement information more clearly.

According to the analysis, 63% of companies chose to register in the US, 37% selected to list in China, and 22% of companies listed in China decided to list in Hong Kong. That is to say, only 15% of companies listed on the mainland of China.

The leading companies, such as Alibaba, Tencent, and Baidu, chose to go public in the U.S. or Hong Kong. The situation depended on various reasons. First, being listed on the U.S or H.K stock market could increase the company reputation. Second, Chinese mainland stock listing requirements would be higher than U.S. and H.K, and the U.S stock market risk assessment was lower than the Chinese market.

Protocol

For this recipe, searching ‘Chinese Internet companies on the stock market’ on google.hk would obtain the list of 55 Chinese Internet Companies which have been listed on the stock market. Further, through searching the company names on the list on google, the basic dataset file were obtained. Importing the basic data file into Raw Graphs would obtain a Dendrograms about the company stock market type, which was then manually adjusted in Adobe Illustrator.
