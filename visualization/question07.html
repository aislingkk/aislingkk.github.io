research question

How about the scale and financial round of the Chinese Internet Unicorns?

Description Protocol Data

Description

The X position represented the company scale, and the Y position represented the company latest financing round. The visualization was about the relationships among company financial round, the scale, the establishment duration, and the valuation data. It indicated that the size of the company personnel was positively related to company valuation. Besides, the scale of the most unicorn companies concentrated in the 100-1000 range. It can be seen from the company founding time and financing round that many unicorn companies quickly reached the D round of financing in recent years. On the one hand, Chinese science and technology sector was developing rapidly in the current decade, with the and the rapid development of venture capital industry, which might lead to the unicorns obtaining many rounds of financing for survival. From another perspective, many new unicorns developed rapidly, which were able to overpass the D round of funding in a short time.

Protocol

The professional database of itjuzi.com was found through the search engine, containing the list of Chinese Internet unicorns. By checking with the Qichacha, the official database for Chinese enterprise, the unicorn dataset files of Chinese Internet companies in different fields were collected. Through the Tableau Prep Builder for cleaning and organizing, the final dataset was obtained. Then, the dataset file was imported into Charticulator and Adobe Illustrator.
