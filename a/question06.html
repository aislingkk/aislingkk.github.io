How about the type and place of the Chinese Internet Unicorns?

Description

The point (circle) was chosen to represent each company. In the visualization, the X position of the circle illustrated the different fields, and the Y position illustrated the different company addresses, which were arranged in alphabetical order. The gradient color of the circle represented the founding company year, the darker the longer. The size of the circle represented the company valuation, the bigger the higher. Therefore, a matrix bubble chart was formed. One circle represented one unicorn company. From the illustration, these unicorn companies mainly concentrated in Chinese economically developed regions, such as Beijing, Shanghai, Zhejiang, and Guangdong.

From the focusing field of the unicorns, which are the most highly valued, the industrial clusterings varied from field to field. For example, DJI, the highest valuation unicorn company in Guangdong Province, belonged to the hardware field. Moreover, Guangdong Province had far more unicorn companies in the hardware field than other regions. It could be inferred that the industrial chain of Guangdong in the hardware manufacturing field was the most complete. While the headquarter of Alibaba located in Hangzhou, the two super unicorns shaped by Alibaba, named Ant Financial and Alibaba Cloud, contributed to the economy of Zhejiang Province. Besides, as the financial capital of China, Shanghai spawned Internet finance companies like Lufax.

Protocol

The professional database of itjuzi.com was found through the search engine, containing the list of Chinese Internet unicorns. By checking with the Qichacha, the official database for Chinese enterprise, the unicorn dataset files of Chinese Internet companies in different fields were collected. Through the Tableau Prep Builder for cleaning and organizing, the final dataset was obtained. Then, the dataset file was imported into Charticulator and Adobe Illustrator.
